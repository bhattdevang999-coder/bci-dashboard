📖 How does NIS Creation work?
▾

What is NIS Creation?

NIS (New Item Setup) generates Amazon-ready listing files from your product data. Upload your pre-upload template (Excel or CSV with styles, colors, sizes, UPCs), and the system generates titles, bullet points, descriptions, and backend keywords — formatted for direct upload to Amazon Vendor Central.

What do I need?

  • Your product data file (the only required input)
  • Optionally: an Amazon NIS template (.xlsm) for direct .xlsm output
  • Optionally: Helium 10 keyword data for search-optimized content
  • Optionally: Brand Analytics data for conversion-optimized content

How it works:

  1. Upload your product data → system auto-detects brand, product types, sizes, colors
  2. Review generated content → each field has a “Why” explanation
  3. Edit, regenerate, or accept each field
  4. Download as CSV or .xlsm files — per style, per category, or all combined

What makes the content smarter over time?

Every edit you make is saved as feedback. After 20+ edits for a brand, the system learns your preferences and generates content that needs fewer corrections.

What is Catalog Health?

Catalog Health checks your Amazon catalog for structural issues that silently kill revenue — orphaned ASINs, broken variation families, missing content, and data gaps.

What do I need?

  • A catalog export from Vendor Central (required)
  • A Sales Diagnostic export (optional — adds revenue impact analysis)

What it checks:

  • Variation integrity: are all children grouped under the correct parent?
  • Content completeness: does every ASIN have a title, 5 bullets, description, images, keywords?
  • Revenue cross-reference: which broken ASINs are costing you money?

What do I do with the results?

Download the Fix File — a CSV with only the broken ASINs and instructions on what to fix. Upload corrections to Vendor Central.

Merge Listings

Fix broken variations, reassign orphaned ASINs, and merge split families

⏳ Coming Q3 2026

What is Merge?

When products are listed under the wrong parent, split across multiple parents, or orphaned with no parent at all — Merge fixes it. The system generates a corrective flat file that reassigns every child ASIN to the correct parent, fixing your variation families.

What can Merge fix?

  • Split families (same style split across 2+ parent ASINs)
  • Orphaned children (ASINs not linked to any parent)
  • Wrong category assignment (shirt listed as shoes)
  • Inconsistent variation themes

How will it work?

1 Upload your catalog data (same as Catalog Health)
2 The system detects all merge issues
3 You review the merge plan — which parent is the “primary” for each family
4 Download a corrective flat file — upload to Vendor Central
5 Re-check next month to confirm merges were applied
📊
In the meantime: Run Catalog Health to identify issues that will need merging.
